G-Flex, T-Mobile USA, 2014

 The first Phablet device launched for T-Mobile USA. Provided unique UX focusing on an immersive viewing experience. Exclusively designed 'fluid UI' allows users to appreciate the world-first curved smartphone.
 I was a sole UX designer for this device, responsible for concepts, wireframes and UX management.

Usability on sizes of a screen, 2011

  I conducted usability testings to study how screen sizes of a mobile device affected usability. Focus of the research was to provide design guidelines to internal UX designers and optimal UI layouts.
 Collaborated with Brain and Research Inc, Korea

Easy UI, 2012

 I conducted user research to evaluate usability and level of preferences of LG’s new UI for first time smartphone buyers. Focus of the research was to figure out users’ perceived level of easiness and its degree of learnability comparing with the standard UI.
  Collaborated with User Centric Inc, USA and Eye Square GMBH, Germany.

Launched with LG Lucid 2 (Verizon Wireless)

Color schemes for a UI, 2011
